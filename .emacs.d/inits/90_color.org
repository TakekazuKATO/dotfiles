** rainbow-mode
数字表記に色つけしてくれる 

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(require 'rainbow-mode)
(add-hook 'css-mode-hook 'rainbow-mode)
(add-hook 'html-mode-hook 'rainbow-mode)
(add-hook 'emacs-lisp-mode-hook 'rainbow-mode)
#+end_src
